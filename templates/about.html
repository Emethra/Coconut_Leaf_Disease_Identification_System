About Coconut Leaf Disease Predictor

Our Mission

Coconut palm trees are vital to tropical agriculture and economy. Early detection of leaf diseases is crucial for maintaining healthy coconut plantations and ensuring sustainable yields. Our AI-powered system provides farmers and agricultural professionals with an easy-to-use tool for rapid disease identification.

Technology Behind the System

Our prediction system is built using state-of-the-art deep learning technology:

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN): Advanced image recognition algorithms
  • TensorFlow/Keras: Industry-standard machine learning framework
  • Transfer Learning: Leveraging pre-trained models for better accuracy
  • Image Preprocessing: Automated image enhancement and standardization

Diseases We Can Detect

Our system is trained to identify the following coconut leaf conditions:

  • Healthy Leaves
  • Red Palm Weevil Damage
  • Rhinoceros Beetle Damage
  • Leaf Spot Disease
  • Bud Rot
  • Black Spot

How to Use the System

  1. Image Quality: Ensure your photo is clear and well-lit
  2. Focus on Leaves: Capture the affected leaf area prominently
  3. File Format: Upload images in PNG, JPG, JPEG, or GIF format
  4. Size Limit: Maximum file size is 16MB
  5. Registration: Create an account to track your prediction history

Important Notes

Disclaimer: This system is designed to assist in disease identification and should not replace professional agricultural consultation. Always consult with agricultural experts for confirmed diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

Accuracy: Our model has been trained on a comprehensive dataset and achieves high accuracy in controlled conditions. However, environmental factors, image quality, and lighting conditions can affect prediction results.
